To all our Salem AG family and community,

As the novel COVID-19 situation continues to impact our lives in ever-changing ways, the safety of our members and community remains of utmost importance. Salem AG has decided to limit in-person gatherings to a minimum to reduce spread of the virus and promote social distancing. The church will be live streaming our Community worship service every Sunday at 9:30am EST. Click here to go to our video page or find us on YouTube at SAG NY.
As a church we continue to promote the goodness and love of God in the midst of this storm and request all to be diligent in prayer and support. God is still sovereign and He will guide us through this. Let us continue to be cautious and use precautions laid out by the government while having full confidence in our God. Our hearts and prayers continue to go out to all the families directly impacted by the virus. May God keep you and lead you in these trying times.
